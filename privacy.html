Primary Privacy Policy

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using our apps, websites, and services. We tried to make it easily readable.

This Privacy Policy applies to all Freely Bee apps, websites, and services (the “Services”) that use your Freely Bee account. Some Services may have additional privacy terms (“Addenda”) to explain data practices specific to that Service.

By creating an account, logging in, or using any Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, you have the option not to use the Services.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Currently:

  • We do not collect personal usage analytics.
  • We do not store your images or files unless you explicitly choose to upload or save them within a Service.
  • We may collect minimal account information (e.g., email, password, display name) to let you log in and use our Services.

In the future:

  • We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our Services.
  • If we do, we will update this Policy before starting.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the Services.
  • Secure your account and prevent misuse.
  • (In the future) Improve features through optional analytics data.

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information.
  • Use your content for advertising purposes.

3. Sharing Information

We may share information only in these situations:

  • With service providers who help us run the Services (e.g., cloud hosting) — and only for that purpose.
  • If required by law (e.g., court order).
  • With your consent.

4. Data Retention

Content you choose to store is kept only as long as you keep it in your account.

Account data is kept until you delete your account, unless required for legal purposes.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll update the date at the top, and if the changes are significant, we’ll let you know before they take effect.

7. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at helper@freelybee.com.
